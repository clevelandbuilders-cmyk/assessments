All 4 Elevations — Full Exterior
37′ × 33′ footprint, 17′ walls
2-story storage building
Removal and disposal of all existing siding and trim on all four elevations of the exterior storage building on property. Application of LP SmartSide lap siding and 1×4 trim boards throughout. Soffit enclosure to seal open eave cavities and eliminate bird infiltration. Building shows extensive rot and paint failure — particularly severe at the base of the west and south elevations.
Site Conditions

Overall Exterior Condition Poor

Extensive deterioration on all four elevations. Paint failure and rot concentrated at the base of all walls. Most severe at west and south elevations where siding is actively separating from the structure.

East Elevation — Boarded Window Flagged

One upper-level window on the east elevation is boarded with plywood. Must be addressed regardless of broader window scope.

Roof & Gutters New 2023

Asphalt shingle roof and gutters replaced in 2023 and in good condition. Not in scope. All work will be performed with care to protect the existing roofline and gutter system.

Bird Infiltration — Upper Eaves Active

Facility staff report birds gaining access through open ceiling joist cavities at the eave line. Soffit enclosure proposed as permanent solution.

Scope of Work
1

Remove & Dispose — All Existing Siding

Full tear-off and disposal of all existing siding material on all four elevations. Existing material is deteriorated and not salvageable. Debris removed from site.

2

Remove & Dispose — All Existing Trim

Full removal and disposal of all existing trim including corner boards, band board, and all window and door casings on all four elevations.

3

Inspect Sheathing & Install House Wrap

Sheathing condition assessed after demo. Water-resistive barrier installed where missing or damaged. Substrate rot repairs scoped separately via change order as needed.

4

Install LP SmartSide Lap Siding

7/16” × 8” × 16′ panels at 6” exposure throughout all four elevations. ~370 pieces including 10% waste.

5

Install LP SmartSide 1×4 Trim Boards

Corner boards at all four corners, horizontal band board at floor-line transition, and casings at all window and door openings. ~38 boards / 527 LF including waste.

6

Install Soffit Enclosure — Bird Infiltration Solution

Soffit material installed along full eave perimeter to permanently close off open joist bays and seal structure against bird entry. Material and footage TBD — measure on site. Performed concurrently with siding scope.

*

Entry Doors — TBD Discuss

Multiple entry doors in poor condition. Once new siding is in place, doors will be visually prominent. Replacement or TLC to be discussed with ownership before quote is finalized.

*

Windows — Not in Scope / Return Visit Optional

Windows not measured at this visit. If replacement desired, return visit required. Ballpark pricing available upon request.

Notes & Next Steps

Substrate Contingency — Change Order Language

Full extent of rot into sheathing and framing cannot be determined until demolition begins. Contract should include a change order provision for substrate/framing repairs discovered during demo — particularly at the base of the west and south elevations where separation is visible.

Siding Color & Finish Selection Needed

LP SmartSide color and finish to be confirmed by ownership before material order can be placed.

Soffit Measurement Required

Eave perimeter measurement and soffit product selection (LP SmartSide, aluminum, or vented panel) needed before soffit scope can be fully priced.

Entry Door Decision

Multiple entry doors are in rough condition. Determine with ownership whether replacement or refurbishment will be included in scope before quote is finalized.

Windows — Optional Return Visit

If window replacement is desired, a return visit for rough opening measurements is required. Ballpark pricing can be provided on request. Boarded east window must be addressed regardless.

Disposal & Site Logistics

Confirm dumpster placement, site access, and any facility-specific logistics with St. Anne Center facilities team before mobilization.
